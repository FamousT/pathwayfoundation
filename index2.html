About Us

SSII - raising shining stars

Shining Stars Initiative International (SSII) now Shining Stars Global Impact Foundation is a mentoring and capacity building forum for teenagers and youths.
Read More

Our Philisophy

Purposeful mentorship and willingness to learn, combined with enabling environment and capacity development; which is believed would put a youth on the right path to success.

Our Vision

To raise purpose and passion driven young men and women to become world phenomena and shining stars in character, conduct and competence.


Our Mission

To impart the teenagers, youths, and women with positive values toward having a right attitude to life.

To groom and mentor teenagers, youths, and women to become responsible, accountable and productive within the society.

To raise qualified individuals who can influence and bring a positive change to the society through their spheres of influence.

To equip the youths and women through capacity development and empowerment projects for economic self reliance.
